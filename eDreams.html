March - September, 2016

UX Research in my Master thesis

When I came back to Zagreb, I started to work on my master thesis and in the following is a short abstract of my work:

In the thesis, a new style of interaction which uses eye tracking device thus replacing traditional input devices is realized. Analysis of relevant research along with some interactive systems based on eye-tracking enabled designing number of new gestures, which has to be conducted by user’s eyes in order to successfully execute specific instruction, thus providing easier and more natural interaction with computer. According to quantitative and qualitative measures acquired during experimental evaluation, it can be concluded that the feedback achieved from test users during usability testing matches assumed meaning of designed gestures.

Remark: I have submitted paper about my master thesis and it has been accepted for poster presentation at ACM womENcourage 2017 conference in Barcelona in September 2017.
